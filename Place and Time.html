Time and Place are major aspects of worldbuilding. If your characters go anywhere or do anything, which they should, you need to know where and when they are going and doing.

White Room Syndrome is the effect of characters that could be in a blank floating space because there is no setting.

In this generator, you will answer questions about your world to recieve details related to your choices, which can then be used to fix a white room.
