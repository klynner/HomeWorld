The Myths and Panthons of created worlds are often an afterthought. This can be a major problem as religion is usually the center of a culture morally, habitually, and traditionally. While this is true, religion is also treated more casually than a modern reader or player tends to realize. Even Tolkien told us how religion is so tied to culture it shows up in the language.

 

For this generator, the gods will not be named, that is the job of the language generator. This is to show you the types of myths available.
